About the Doctor

Meet Dr. Rahul Sharma

MBBS MD – Internal Medicine General Physician Diabetes Specialist

Dr. Rahul Sharma is a highly qualified and compassionate physician with over 10 years of clinical experience in general medicine and chronic disease management. Having served thousands of patients across Guwahati and Assam, he is known for his patient-first approach and thoroughness in diagnosis.

After completing his MBBS and MD from a prestigious institution, Dr. Sharma established Sharma Health Clinic with a mission to bring affordable, world-class healthcare to every family in Guwahati. His approach combines evidence-based treatment with genuinely personalized care tailored to each patient's unique needs and lifestyle.

He specializes in diabetes management, hypertension, preventive care, and general consultations — always taking the time to listen, explain, and empower his patients toward better health outcomes.

Our Services

Comprehensive Medical Care

We offer a wide range of healthcare services designed to keep you and your family healthy at every stage of life.

General Consultation

Expert diagnosis and treatment for common illnesses, fevers, infections, coughs, colds, and general health concerns — for all age groups.

Diabetes Management

Comprehensive diabetic care including blood sugar monitoring, personalized diet guidance, medication management, and complication prevention.

Blood Pressure Treatment

Expert hypertension management with lifestyle counselling, medication, and regular monitoring to protect your heart and overall vascular health.

Health Checkups

Thorough preventive health examinations, blood tests, and full-body screening packages tailored to your age, gender, and health goals.

Preventive Care

Proactive health strategies including vaccinations, nutritional guidance, stress management counseling, and early disease detection programs.

Chronic Disease Care

Long-term management of thyroid disorders, respiratory issues, and other chronic conditions with regular follow-ups and treatment adjustments.
